Making GovHack Data Accessible
http://hackanation.tk/
Correl8 has conceptualised to collaborate with Hacknation team who have developed a sophisticated data crawler to use similar code from the crawler to accumulate all availabe data sets on data.gov.au and to improve the data availability for the - Dash - project. This will ensure a larger library of data sets can be accessed by users to interrogate and evaluate at leisure. This collaboration with ensure that all data sets are readily accessible and fully inclusive for all users regardless of their expertise in coding. Correl8 will successfully achieve the aims of - Dash - with this collaboration.
